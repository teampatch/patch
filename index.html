Problem

200 million people are affected by Tuberculosis worldwide. There has been a cure for over 70 years, still more than 1.5 million people die from the disease annually. Largely due to prohibitive treatement costs.

The Solution

Patch.

Patch utilizes proven transdermal patch technology to autonomously deliver Tuberculosis drug treatments over the course of a 12 month period.

Cost Effective

On average in the USA, the minimum cost to cure a case of tuberculosis is $31,000. This is entirely thanks to the inefficency of the American Healthcare System. Patch reduces this cost down to approx. $3,100. This shocking 90% cost decrease is due to the fact that Patch cuts the healthcare system nearly entirely out of the equation. Delivering treatments steadily and autonomously for a period of 12 months.

Better Treatment

Patch uses Transdermal patch technology, which enables treatements to enter the blood stream way faster than traditional pill treatments, while also guaranteeing a higher degree of treatement thanks to the fact that pills will often expirience pre-mature metabolism in the digestive process.
